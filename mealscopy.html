Diets and Meal preps

Having a consistent diet and eating cleaner is an important part of the fitness and healthy lifestyle process and can look different for everyone! One of the easiest ways to stay on track when eating cleaner is to meal prep ahead and also only have snacks that are healthy in your home instead of junk!

Picking What's Best for You


Different "Diets"

I don't usually like using the word diet when it comes to eating healthy because people usually think of "restrictions" when they hear diet and think they won't enjoy the food because it's healthy and boring which does not have to be the case! We can call them prefrences so it doesn't sound as bad :)

Some different meal prefrences may include:

  • Being Vegetarian
  • Being Vegan
  • Keto
  • No dairy
  • And much more!

I can't give too much information on nutrition because I'm not a nutritionist but there are many resources online to find what diet or "prefrence" works best for you. Also this is YOUR journey so what works for me may not work for you and that's okay! Under my INFLUENCER page, I have linked some influencers Youtube Channels that have videos on what they recommend for nutrition!
