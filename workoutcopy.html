WORKOUT!

When working out you can really customize your workouts with things you love. This may include just going for a walk and/or run, swimming, biking, hiking, and more!

What you need

Whether you're working out at home or at the gym, there a few things that you can add to your routine and can help improve your experience

  1. Have water at all times!
  2. Headphones and music you enjoy
  3. Resistence Bands
  4. Floor mat
  5. Fitness gloves for lifting
  6. Barbell padding for lift
  7. Lifting belt for back support

(These are just recommeneded to help and enhance your workout experience but not needed (besides water, DRINK YOUR WATER!)

Targeting Areas

I personally love going to the gym to work on making myself fit and stronger! When working out with weights you can really focus on specific muscle groups in your workout that you want to work on and make stronger. These muscle groups include:

  • Biceps
  • Back
  • Quads
  • Glutes
  • And much more!

Weekly routine

Having a routine prepped and ready for the week with your weekly workouts is a good step to take to stay motivated. Having your days planned to specifc muscle groups and workouts can help simlify working out at the gym when you get there so you don't have wonder what to do or worry about thinking of workouts on the spot!
